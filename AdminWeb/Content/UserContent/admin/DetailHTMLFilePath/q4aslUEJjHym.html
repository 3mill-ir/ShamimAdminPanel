The purpose of the goals is in fact the results of the implementation of the programs and strategies of the park, which should be appropriate to the vision, mission, and internal and environmental conditions of the organization and in such a way as to be quantitative. Following are the park's objectives as follows:

The growth of the province's technology in competition with neighboring countries
Increasing the share of medium and high value added goods in the GDP of the province and developing the province's industry
Growth and development of knowledge economy in the province
Reducing dependency on state budget
